About Me and the Design

Hello, I'm Mohamed Hashi, a professional designer with a focus on both clothing and utensil designs, including custom phone cases, laptop covers, and more. I have always been fascinated by creating visual art that complements personal style. Whether it’s a t-shirt or a phone case, I aim to make every design unique and personalized.

Somali Design Center specializes in creating unique and innovative designs for both clothing and utensils. Our clothing range spans from casual streetwear to bespoke fashion, while our utensil designs include custom mugs, phone cases, laptop covers, water bottles, notebooks, and more. We aim to transform everyday items into artistic expressions that reflect personality, style, and Somali heritage. Whether it’s through wearable fashion or personalized utensils, Somali Design Hub is here to collaborate and bring your vision to life with creativity and cultural pride.
